# 08 - Investor

Investors provide the capital for the platform.

## Key Capabilities
- **Wallet Management:** Submit deposit requests to fund their wallet, and submit withdrawal requests from their ROI balance.
- **Investing:** Allocate wallet balance into active investments that generate a fixed monthly return. (Note: missing frontend UI for this).
- **KYC:** Submit ID documents for verification.

## Limitations
- Cannot refer other investors.
- Cannot process their own withdrawals or deposits; all require Admin approval.
- Currently missing frontend implementations for Top-ups, ROI History, and Investment History.
